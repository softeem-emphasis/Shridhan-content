Shridhan provides turnkey pressure piping solutions, including design, fabrication, and commissioning. Piping systems are engineered to meet ASME, IS, and other design codes, ensuring compliance with global safety and performance standards.
Piping is available up to 8" diameter with a pressure rating of up to 2500 lb. Systems are suitable for a wide range of operating conditions, from full vacuum to 350 kg/cm² and -40°C to +450°C.
Welding is executed by ASME Section IX certified welders, and supported by in-house NDT testing facilities, ensuring high-quality fabrication.
